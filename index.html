Comprehensive Disaster Management Features

From prediction to recovery, our AI-powered platform provides end-to-end disaster management capabilities for modern emergency response teams.

AI Risk Prediction

Advanced machine learning algorithms analyze weather patterns, historical data, and geographical factors to predict disaster risks with 95% accuracy.

Real-Time Detection

Integrate multiple data sources including satellite imagery, IoT sensors, and citizen reports for immediate incident detection and verification.

Smart Resource Allocation

AI-optimized resource deployment considering traffic conditions, vehicle capacity, and incident severity for maximum efficiency.

Multi-Channel Alerts

Broadcast emergency alerts via SMS, mobile apps, sirens, and social media in multiple languages with location-based targeting.

Citizen App Integration

Mobile application for citizens to report incidents, receive alerts, locate shelters, and access emergency services with offline capabilities.

Scalable Architecture

Cloud-based infrastructure that scales from district to national level with integration capabilities for IMD, ISRO, and international disaster networks.
